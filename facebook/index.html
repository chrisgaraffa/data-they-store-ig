What They Know: Facebook

Introduction

Thanks to mass pressure and legal changes, most social networks let us export the data they have on us. This tool for your Facebook export will help you understand what information they have about you - including how many times you've changed your profile or your password, where you've logged in from, what they think your interests are and more.

Instructions

First, you need to download a copy of your Facebook data. It could take a day or two for Facebook to prepare it. They'll send you an email with a link to a file with the extension .zip.
Open that file on your computer and it will give you a folder. Drag that folder to the Browse button below, or click the button and select it.
